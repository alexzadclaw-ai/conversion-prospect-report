## Main issue
Practice information is present, but the dated page structure makes appointment booking and patient trust cues less prominent.

## Recommended surgical improvements
- Do not rebrand; keep existing blue dental look and doctor/practice imagery.
- Make “Schedule Appointment” the clear primary CTA on desktop/mobile.
- Add new-patient reassurance and insurance/comfort cues above the fold.
- Reduce early-page link clutter and create one appointment path.

## Suggested prototype direction
Use existing logo/photography and preserve the current brand feel. Add only: stronger CTA hierarchy, short above-the-fold lead form, trust strip, mobile sticky call/estimate bar, and service-specific routing.

## Why this is pitch-worthy
This is a low-risk, measurable conversion improvement. It does not require rebuilding the entire website and can be A/B tested or launched as a landing-page variant.
